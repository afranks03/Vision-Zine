● Feature 01 ● Editor's Letter ● A. Franks ● Brooklyn, May 2026
The OpeningA Manifesto in Plain Ink

I am Adrian Franks. The work is the heartbeat.

Thirty-one years woven through design, advertising, and the arts. Atlanta first, then New York for eighteen of them, and now a second home growing out of Athens, Georgia. I stand at forty-nine and I do not see a finish line — I see a runway.

I am a financially successful commercial artist building with the brands that move culture. I am an investor turning property into engines of B2B enterprise. I am a creative technologist building platforms that bridge talent, ideas, and opportunity for the community I call home. I am the man in the best shape of his adult life. I am the husband. I am the father. I am the legacy in motion.

Every brushstroke is intentional. Every digital pixel a piece of a larger masterpiece. This issue lays it down — what I have built, what I am building, what I will not stop building until it stands.

And the second half of this issue belongs to Nicole. Because no version of this future is mine alone.

— A.d. Editor-in-Chief
Brooklyn × Athens

This is Issue I. We update it once a year. Whenever you are ready.

● Feature 10 ● Closing Note ● A. Franks

For Nicole.

There is no version of this future that is mine alone. Eighteen years in a city that does not give itself easily. A marriage that has held since 2007. A son here. A child in spirit. Two homes. Two studios. The book we have been writing together this whole time.

Every brushstroke on the canvas of this life is intentional. Every digital pixel a piece of the larger masterpiece. The studio in Athens. The list we are building. The lifestyle and the editorial and the venues and the women you are designing for. The boy we are raising. The version of me who will rise to meet the next decade — strong, present, free.

I am Adrian Franks. A creative force. A community builder. A family man. A beacon of innovation and excellence. The future is not just a continuation of the past — it is a bold declaration of what I am destined to leave behind.

And what I leave behind, I leave with you.
